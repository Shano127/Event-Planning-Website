ABOUT US

We Make Your Day More Special!

Starlight was created with the notion that all events should be unique and thoughtful. Our team has worked in the Portland area for over 15 years in weddings and special events. We’ve done everything from an orchard country wedding of 20 people, to managing high-touch corporate galas and multi-day events. Our foundation of events is built on providing a full-service and freshly creative team to our special clients. Truth – we love what we do, and take pride in creating experiences for our clients!

OUR SERVICES

YOU WILL GET FOLLOWING SERVICES FROM US.

Event Planning and Coordination Service.

At EventPlannerHub, we specialize in full-service event planning and coordination. Our experienced team will work closely with you to understand your vision and bring it to life. From venue selection and décor to catering and entertainment, we handle every detail to ensure a seamless and memorable event for you and your guests.


Event Promotion and Marketing.

Maximize attendance and engagement with EventPlannerHub's comprehensive event promotion and marketing services. Our team will develop a tailored marketing strategy to reach your target audience, utilizing social media campaigns, email marketing, and influencer partnerships to generate buzz and drive ticket sales for your event.



Event Photography and Videography

Capture every moment of your event with EventPlannerHub's professional photography and videography services. Our skilled photographers and videographers will document your event from start to finish, providing high-quality images and videos that you can cherish for years to come.
